## How to Use KOISK

### Login or Create Account

**First Time?**
1. Click "Sign up"
2. Enter name, email, username, password
3. Click "Create Account"
4. Check your email (optional verification link)

**Returning User?**
1. Click "Sign in"
2. Enter username and password
3. Click "Sign in"

### Pay Your Bill

1. From dashboard, click the utility card
2. Click "Pay Bill"
3. Enter details from your bill:
   - Consumer/Meter number
   - Billing period (e.g., Feb-2026)
   - Amount
   - Payment method
4. Click "Submit"
5. Complete payment in secure gateway
6. You'll see confirmation

**Expected time**: 2-5 minutes

### Request New Connection

1. Click the utility card
2. Click "New Connection"
3. Fill in your information
4. Upload required documents (ID, address proof)
5. Click "Submit"
6. Department will review and contact you

**Expected time**: 5-7 working days

### Report a Problem

**Water Leak?**
1. Click "Water" card
2. Click "Report Leak"
3. Describe the location and severity
4. Submit

**Department will respond**: Within 24 hours

### Track Your Requests

- Go to "Recent Requests" section
- Click any request to see:
  - Current status
  - Complete history
  - Timeline
  - Any messages

## Understanding Request Status

| Status | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **DRAFT** | You're still creating the request (can edit) |
| **SUBMITTED** | We received your request |
| **PROCESSING** | Department is reviewing it |
| **APPROVED** | ✅ Approved! Proceeding |
| **COMPLETED** | ✅ Done! Check email for details |
| **REJECTED** | ❌ Couldn't complete. Check the reason |

## Help & Troubleshooting

### I Can't Login

**Check these**:
- Username is spelled correctly
- Password is correct (CapsLock off)
- Account exists (did you sign up?)
- Try "Forgot password" if needed

**Still stuck?** Contact support

### Payment Failed

**Reasons**:
- Not enough money in account
- Card expired
- Wrong card details
- Temporary gateway issue

**Try**:
- Use different payment method
- Try again in 10 minutes
- Check with your bank
- Contact support

### I Didn't Get Confirmation Email

**Check**:
- Spam/junk folder
- Wait 5-10 minutes
- Try requesting email again
- Contact support

### Request Still Pending

**Normal times**:
- Bill payment: 5 minutes
- Service request: 3-7 days
- Department approval: 1-3 days

**Check status** anytime in your dashboard

### How Do I Reset My Password?

1. Click "Forgot password?"
2. Enter your email
3. Click link in email
4. Create new password
5. Login with new password

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is KOISK Safe?

**Yes!** We use:
- Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
- Secure password storage
- PCI-compliant payment processing
- Regular security updates
- No data sharing

### Who Can I Use KOISK With?

Anyone with:
- Valid utility account
- Email address
- Internet connection
- Any modern browser

### Can I Pay Someone Else's Bill?

Not yet. Currently only account owners can pay. Coming soon: pay for family members' accounts.

### What if I Pay Twice by Mistake?

The system checks for duplicates. If it happens, you'll get an automatic refund within 3-5 days.

### Can I Cancel a Request?

**Yes**, if it's:
- DRAFT status (not yet submitted) - edit or delete
- SUBMITTED status - contact support to cancel

**No**, if it's:
- APPROVED or later - proceed with it

### Why Do I Need to Upload Documents?

For new connections and service transfers, we need proof of:
- Your identity (driver's license, passport)
- Your address (utility bill, rental agreement)

This is required by law.

### What if the Department Rejects My Request?

You'll see the reason in your dashboard. You can:
- Fix the issue and resubmit
- Contact department for clarification
- Reach out to customer support

## Security Best Practices

✅ **DO:**
- Use a strong password (mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
- Logout when finished

- Report suspicious activity immediately

❌ **DON'T:**
- Share your password
- Use same password everywhere
- Leave browser unattended
- Use public WiFi for payments
- Save payment info on shared devices

## Glossary
