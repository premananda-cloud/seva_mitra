# KOISK - Manage Your Utilities Online
## A Modern Platform for Electricity, Water & Gas Services

Welcome to KOISK! This is your complete solution for managing utility bills and services online. Whether you need to pay your electricity bill, report a water leak, or request a new gas connection, KOISK makes it simple and secure.

---

## What is KOISK?

KOISK (Kiosk for Utility Services) is a digital platform that brings government utility services online. Instead of visiting offices or waiting in queues, you can now:

✅ **Pay Bills** - Pay electricity, water, or gas bills online  
✅ **Request Services** - Apply for new connections or service transfers  
✅ **Track Status** - Monitor your requests in real-time  
✅ **Submit Complaints** - Report issues like water leaks instantly  
✅ **View History** - Keep track of all your payments and requests

## How It Works

### Step-by-Step Example: Paying Your Electricity Bill

1. **Login** to your account
2. **Click "Electricity"** card on dashboard
3. **Click "Pay Bill"**
4. **Enter Details**:
   - Meter Number
   - Billing Period
   - Amount
   - Payment Method
5. **Click "Submit"**
6. **Confirm Payment** in your payment gateway
7. **Check Status** - Your request shows "Processing" then "Completed"

### Request Status Explained

| Status | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **DRAFT** | You're still filling in details |
| **SUBMITTED** | Your request has been sent |
| **PROCESSING** | The department is working on it |
| **APPROVED** | Your request has been approved |
| **COMPLETED** | Done! Check your email for confirmation |
| **REJECTED** | Something went wrong - check the details |

## Common Tasks

### Pay a Bill

1. Click on the utility card (Electricity, Water, or Gas)
2. Click "Pay Bill"
3. Enter:
   - Consumer number (from your bill)
   - Billing period
   - Amount due
   - Payment method
4. Click "Pay Now"
5. Complete payment in secure gateway

**Expected time**: 2-5 minutes

### Report a Water Leak

1. Click "Water" card
2. Click "Report Leak"
3. Fill in:
   - Consumer number
   - Leak location (e.g., "near meter", "street outside house")
   - Severity (High = broken pipe, Medium = steady leak, Low = dripping)
   - Description
4. Click "Submit Report"

**Expected time**: Department will contact you within 24 hours

### Request New Connection

1. Click on the utility (Electricity, Water, or Gas)
2. Click "New Connection"
3. Fill in your details:
   - Full name
   - Address
   - Proof documents (photo ID, proof of address)
4. Click "Submit Request"
5. Department will review and contact you

**Expected time**: 5-7 working days

## Troubleshooting

### I forgot my password

1. Click "Forgot password?" on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Click link in the email you receive
4. Set a new password
5. Login with new password

### I can't login

**Check**:
- Username is correct (case-sensitive)
- Password is correct
- CAPS LOCK is not on
- Your account exists

**If still stuck**: Contact support

### Payment failed

**Common reasons**:
- Insufficient funds
- Card expired
- Wrong card details
- Payment gateway temporary issue

**Try**:
- Use a different payment method
- Try again in 10 minutes
- Check with your bank
- Contact support

### I didn't receive confirmation email

- Check spam/junk folder
- Wait 5-10 minutes
- Try requesting email again
- Contact support

## FAQs

### Q: Is KOISK secure?
**A**: Yes! KOISK uses industry-standard encryption and security measures. Your data and payments are protected.

### Q: What if I make a mistake in my request?
**A**: If your request is still in DRAFT status, you can edit it. Once submitted, contact support to modify.

### Q: How long do requests take?
**A**: 
- Bill payments: 2-5 minutes
- Complaints: 24-48 hours for response
- New connections: 5-7 working days
- Service transfers: 3-5 working days

### Q: What if I pay the same bill twice?
**A**: The system checks for duplicate payments. If it happens, you'll receive a refund automatically.

### Q: Can I cancel a request?
**A**: Yes, but only if it's in DRAFT or SUBMITTED status. Once approved, contact the department.

### Q: What payment methods are available?
**A**: Credit cards, debit cards, UPI, net banking, and mobile wallets.

### Q: Is there a mobile app?
**A**: Not yet, but the website works great on mobile phones!

### Q: Who maintains KOISK?
**A**: KOISK is run by the utility department with government oversight.
